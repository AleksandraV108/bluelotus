Spa body massages and treatments

With elements of eastern innovation, exotic fragrances and natural products, Blue Lotus Spa promises wellness experiences that will revive your spirit and awaken your senses.

How everything began

The brand Blue Lotus Spa was born in 2001 when founder Jennifer Anntons with passion for sharing well-being with those in her community opened the first Blue Lotus Day Spa in the charming street Abbey Road 23 in London. With a years of good tradition of thousand satisfied guests, Blue Lotus Spa is today one of most famous Spa centers in London.

What to expect in our Spa salon?

We all need time to relax, breathe, stretch and zone out and that is a feeling we want you to experience while spending your time in our warm and beautiful environment specially designed to help you relax and to forget all your worries.

We are providing various services for men and women as well as for couples and groups. To meet your special needs we have in our offer special packages like a full day spa packages , best couples massage deals and we also offer spa vouchers and gifts that are valid for a full 12 months. The products and oils we are using are all pure natural and organic.
