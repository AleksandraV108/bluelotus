Therapeutic stone body massage

Hot stone therapy is a specialist massage that uses smooth, flat, heated basalt stones that are positioned along your spine, in the palms of your hands, along your legs and between the toes. During this therapeutic hot stone massage the stones are placed on your body, they help you to go into a state of relaxation–both physically and mentally.

When your muscles are warm, they respond better to massage techniques like kneading and rolling which helps to release deep tension and ease sore muscles. The therapeutic hot stone massage helps in improving your circulation, with symptoms of depression and anxiety, it improve sleep and much more .

hydrotherapy

Hydrotherapy is a form of massage which combines hot water and oil to enhance muscle tissue. It is known since ancient times to help treat a wide range of ailments such as high blood pressure, anxiety, aching joints, and sore muscles. It helps calm the senses, and rejuvenate the body. Immersing yourself in water between 28 and 40 degrees activates the blood circulation and the body, suspended in the water, relaxes completely. Stress is relieved and the mind and body are calmed.
